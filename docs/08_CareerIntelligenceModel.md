# Career Copilot — Career Reasoning Model

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines how Career Copilot transforms raw career information into structured professional understanding.

Rather than directly generating resumes, the system first reasons about a candidate's professional experiences before producing any career assets.

The Career Reasoning Model serves as the intelligence engine behind every AI capability within Career Copilot.

---

# 2. Reasoning Philosophy

Career Copilot does not reason over resumes.

It reasons over professional experiences.

It does not optimize language before understanding meaning.

Every generated output must originate from authentic evidence through a transparent reasoning process.

Reasoning always comes before generation.

---

# 3. The Seven-Stage Reasoning Pipeline

Every reasoning task follows the same pipeline.

```text
Experience / Job Description
            │
            ▼
1. Semantic Parsing
            │
            ▼
2. Fact Extraction
            │
            ▼
3. Business Interpretation
            │
            ▼
4. Capability Reasoning
            │
            ▼
5. Opportunity Matching
            │
            ▼
6. Professional Expression
            │
            ▼
7. Interview Validation
```

Each stage enriches information while preserving authenticity.

---

# 4. Stage 1 — Semantic Parsing

## Objective

Understand the meaning of natural language.

This stage converts free text into semantic components without making judgments.

Typical elements include:

- Actor
- Action
- Object
- Context
- Scope
- Outcome

No capability inference occurs at this stage.

---

# 5. Stage 2 — Fact Extraction

## Objective

Extract structured facts from parsed information.

Facts represent objective information supported by evidence.

Typical facts include:

- Team size
- Product
- Industry
- Geography
- Timeline
- Technologies
- Business metrics

Only verifiable facts are extracted.

No interpretation is performed.

---

# 6. Stage 3 — Business Interpretation

## Objective

Interpret what the experience represents from a business perspective.

Career Copilot reasons about business activities rather than resume sentences.

Typical interpretation dimensions include:

- Business Goal
- Business Problem
- Business Context
- Stakeholders
- Operational Scope
- Business Impact
- Complexity

Business interpretation creates the foundation for capability reasoning.

---

# 7. Stage 4 — Capability Reasoning

## Objective

Infer professional capabilities demonstrated by the experience.

Capabilities are inferred from business interpretation rather than directly from text.

Examples include:

- Leadership
- Product Thinking
- Strategy
- Execution
- Stakeholder Management
- Cross-functional Collaboration
- Data-driven Decision Making

Every inferred capability must reference supporting evidence.

---

# 8. Stage 5 — Opportunity Matching

## Objective

Compare inferred capabilities against target opportunity requirements.

Matching is based on capability alignment rather than keyword similarity.

The system evaluates:

- Core capability alignment
- Transferable capabilities
- Missing capabilities
- Risk factors
- Overall feasibility

This stage determines whether authentic resume optimization should proceed.

---

# 9. Stage 6 — Professional Expression

## Objective

Translate authentic capabilities into professional communication.

Possible outputs include:

- Resume Bullet
- Tailored Resume
- Cover Letter
- LinkedIn Summary
- Professional Biography

Expression never introduces new facts.

It only improves communication.

---

# 10. Stage 7 — Interview Validation

## Objective

Verify that every generated statement can be explained by the candidate.

Interview preparation validates:

- Evidence
- Capability
- Narrative
- Behavioral examples

The objective is not only to generate documents but also to ensure the candidate is prepared to defend every statement.

---

# 11. Core Reasoning Rules

The Career Reasoning Model follows several mandatory rules.

## Evidence Before Reasoning

Reasoning must always originate from authentic evidence.

---

## Reasoning Before Generation

Professional language may only be generated after reasoning is complete.

---

## Complete Traceability

Every capability must be traceable to supporting evidence.

---

## Explainability

Every recommendation should include a reasoning path.

---

## Authenticity

Reasoning may reorganize information.

It must never fabricate information.

---

# 12. Future Evolution

The Career Reasoning Model is designed to support future products beyond resume optimization.

Future applications include:

- Career Strategy
- Learning Recommendations
- Promotion Planning
- Internal Mobility
- Networking Preparation
- Personal Branding

All future reasoning should follow the same reasoning pipeline.

---

# 13. Key Takeaway

Career Copilot does not generate resumes.

It builds a structured understanding of professional experience through reasoning before producing any career asset.

Reasoning is the foundation of every capability within the system.
